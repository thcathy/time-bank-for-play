Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2024

Overview

Earn Time To Play ("the App") is designed to help you balance productivity with entertainment by tracking focus time versus play time. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Key Point: All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Information We Collect

The App collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Time Entries: Records of your focus time and play time sessions
  • Settings: Your app preferences (theme, language, time ratio settings)
  • Timer State: Current stopwatch state to resume timing after app restart

This data is stored using your device's local storage and is never transmitted to any external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, process, or store:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Any data from other apps on your device

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SharedPreferences: For app settings and preferences
  • Hive Database: For time entry records

Your data remains on your device and is not synced to any cloud service. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be deleted.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because:

  • We do not collect any data from your device
  • We do not have access to your locally stored data
  • The App does not contain any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

Data Export (Share Feature)

The App includes a feature to export/share your time tracking data. When you use this feature:

  • You have full control over what data is shared and with whom
  • The sharing is performed using your device's native sharing functionality
  • We do not have access to or store any shared data

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally and we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View your data anytime within the App
  • Delete: Clear your data through the App settings or by uninstalling the App
  • Export: Use the share feature to export your data
